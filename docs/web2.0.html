Google Search & PageRank

  • Who: Larry Page and Sergey Brin, founders of Google.
  • What: They developed the PageRank algorithm to rank web pages by relevance.
  • Why: Their goal was to improve the quality of search results on the web.
  • When: Google was founded in 1998, and PageRank became a key feature.
  • How: PageRank used the number and quality of links to rank web pages, which revolutionized search engines.
